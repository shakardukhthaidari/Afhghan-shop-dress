Who we are ?

AFGHAN SHOPS,
Afghan Collection began in 2010 as a small part-time business selling fine clothes,
jewelry, accessories and other items.
Since then the business has grown and has concentrated on fashion selling mainly clothes,
jewelry and accessories. We directly buy from Afghanistan,
we personally meet each vendor to assure good quality by choosing the finest fabrics, materials, and craftsmanship. Zeba Afghan collection sells retail and wholesale,
accepting all custom orders. A proportion of our sales go to the needy people in Afghanistan.
